Research Accounts

Ever Nova Life supplies materials for qualified in-vitro research. Organizations may be asked to provide institutional or business information before ordering.

How Approval Works

Access is granted to qualified organizations rather than by a single checkbox at checkout.

  1. Application submitted — you complete the short form below.
  2. Organization reviewed — we review the business or institutional information provided.
  3. Account approved or declined — you receive a decision by email.
  4. Ordering access enabled — approved accounts can view ordering options after signing in.

Please do not submit confidential research protocols through this form. A general description of your intended research context is sufficient.

Apply for Research Access

Complete the form and submit — it opens a pre-filled message in your email app addressed to our research-accounts team. You can also email support@evernovalife.com directly.
