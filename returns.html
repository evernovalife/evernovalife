30-Day Returns

We stand behind our products with a straightforward 30-day return policy.

Your satisfaction matters to us. We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on eligible products, so if something is not right you can request a return within 30 days of delivery.

Return Eligibility

To qualify for a return, your item must meet all of the following conditions:

  • Unopened and unused.
  • In its original, sealed packaging.
  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase, such as your order number or receipt.
  • Opened vials and reconstituted product are NOT returnable for safety and integrity reasons.

How to Start a Return

Starting a return is simple — just follow these steps:

  • Contact our team at support@evernovalife.com with your order number.
  • Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return instructions.
  • Ship the item back to us in its original sealed packaging.
  • Once received and inspected, your refund is issued within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Refunds

Approved refunds cover the purchase price of the returned product. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is the result of our error, and return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless otherwise stated.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery and include photos of the product and packaging. We will arrange a free replacement at no cost to you.

Non-Returnable Items

For safety and integrity reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened vials.
  • Bacteriostatic water once opened.
  • Clearance or final-sale items.

Still have questions about a return? Our team is happy to help.
