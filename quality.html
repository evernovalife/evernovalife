Quality & Documentation

Each released lot is supported by identity and purity documentation. Below is how material is tested, how a lot is released, and where to find lot-specific records.

Our Testing Approach

Material offered by Ever Nova Life is analyzed by an independent laboratory, and the original reports are published in the COA Library below.

What is testedIdentity and content (mg) for every listed lot; purity (%) is reported for single-peptide lots. Blend lots are analyzed for the identity and content of each component (purity not included).
LaboratoryJanoshik Analytical (janoshik.com) — an independent third-party laboratory. Each report is verifiable by its task number at janoshik.com.

The reports below are original third-party laboratory documents, provided in addition to and clearly distinguished from any internal release notes.

Our Lot-Release Process

Four steps every item passes through before it becomes available.

1

Material Received

Each product is received and recorded as a numbered production batch from the manufacturing source.

2

Lot Identified

The batch is identified by its manufacturer batch number (e.g. CS-mc10-0408), tying every unit to a single production run.

3

Documentation Reviewed

The batch's independent Janoshik report — identity, measured content, and purity for single-peptide lots — is checked and published in the COA Library below.

4

Lot Released

A product is listed for order once its batch is matched to a published, independently verifiable third-party report.
