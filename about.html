About Ever Nova Life

Ever Nova Life supplies documented research materials to qualified laboratories. Our release process focuses on lot traceability, document review, and clear technical specifications.

Our Story

Ever Nova Life was founded by a small group of laboratory researchers who kept running into the same frustrating problem: peptide quality that varied wildly from supplier to supplier and from lot to lot. Reagents that should have behaved identically gave inconsistent results, and there was rarely any documentation to explain why.

We built Ever Nova Life to fix that. The "nest" in our name is deliberate — we wanted to create a safe, dependable home for trusted compounds, where every product is treated like it belongs in a serious research program. No mystery vials, no hand-waving, no surprises.

That commitment shows up in how we operate. Released lots are analyzed by an independent laboratory, and the original reports are published in our COA Library so you can review lot-specific identity, measured content, and available purity documentation before you order. We pair that with tracked U.S. fulfillment.

All products are sold strictly for in-vitro research and laboratory use only. We take that boundary seriously, and we expect the researchers we serve to do the same.
